Refresh the app's memory from your driving history

Why we need this upload

Uploading your platform history is what gives Driver Toolkit its memory. It powers wait-time estimates, start-area comparisons, low-value area warnings, journey-regret analysis, and earnings leak detection.

Without this file, the app can still handle expenses, targets, and settings, but your recommendations will be limited.

How to get your Uber data

  1. 1

    Go to Uber Privacy Portal

    Navigate to myprivacy.uber.com. On mobile, switch your browser to Desktop Site first.

  2. 2

    Request your data

    Select Driver data and wait for the email. Uber can take 24–72 hours.

  3. 3

    Download the ZIP

    Save the raw ZIP file directly to your phone files. No need to unzip it first.

  4. 4

    Upload here

    Upload the raw file below to refresh your insights, bad bets, and reports.

Currently supports Uber Privacy ZIP. Bolt and Lyft are planned next.
